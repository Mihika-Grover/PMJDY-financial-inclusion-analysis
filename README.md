


PMJDY Financial Inclusion Dashboard
Analysing the quality of financial inclusion under PM Jan Dhan Yojana


Data Sources
•	PMJDY Statewise Data - pmjdy.gov.in (36 states/UTs)
•	Census 2011 - NDAP (aggregated from village-level to state)
•	NFHS-5 - NDAP (filtered and averaged across Rural/Urban)
•	RBI Handbook of Statistics on Indian States

Key Findings
•	Mean RuPay activation ratio: 0.676 (i.e., ~32% of accounts are likely dormant)
•	Women's mobile access is the strongest predictor of activation quality
•	Kerala & Mizoram fall in the lowest tier despite high literacy — pre-existing bank penetration makes PMJDY accounts redundant
•	Bihar, Manipur, MP showed the largest gains in women's bank usage (+51%, +40%, +38%) driven by DBT transfers
